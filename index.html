The rules of the game are simple

Hold down the spraycan to paint the wall and release when you want to move to the next section.

At random intervals PC Plod will appear and try to catch you in the act! Make sure you're not spraying when he is looking, he'll only give you a few chances!

Top Tip... Each section is about 12-13% so as soon as your're done move on to the next bit!
